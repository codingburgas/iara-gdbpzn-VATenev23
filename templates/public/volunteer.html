Why Volunteer?

Volunteer firefighters are the backbone of our community. You'll receive:

  • ✅ Free professional training
  • ✅ Equipment and gear provided

Requirements
Basic Requirements:
  • Age 18 or older
  • Physically fit
  • Live or work in Burgas area
  • Clean criminal record
  • Team player attitude
Training Provided:
  • Fire suppression
  • First aid/CPR
  • Vehicle rescue
  • Hazmat awareness
  • Equipment operation
