Fire Safety Tips

Protect yourself, your family, and your home

🔥 In Case of Fire
  1. GET OUT - Leave immediately, don't grab belongings
  2. STAY LOW - Crawl under smoke
  3. CHECK DOORS - Feel with back of hand before opening
  4. CALL 112 - From outside, after you're safe
  5. NEVER go back inside
🏠 Home Prevention
  • Test smoke alarms monthly
  • Change batteries twice a year
  • Keep fire extinguishers in kitchen
  • Create escape plan with family
  • Practice fire drills at home
🍳 Kitchen Safety
  • Never leave cooking unattended
  • Keep flammable items away from stove
  • Turn pot handles inward
  • Keep a lid nearby to smother grease fires
⚡ Electrical Safety
  • Don't overload outlets
  • Replace damaged cords
  • Space heaters need 3 feet of space
  • Call electrician if lights flicker
