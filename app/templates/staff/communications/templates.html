📖 How to Use Templates

Templates appear as Quick Reply buttons in the incident chat page.

Click a template to automatically send that message to all units assigned to the incident.

Pro tip: Use {{ eta }} or other variables in templates - they will be replaced with actual values!

Example templates:
  • "En Route. ETA: {{ eta }} minutes."
  • "Arrived on scene. Assessing situation."
  • "Need backup at this location."
